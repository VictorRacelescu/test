Passions

Since childhood, tennis has been my passion. From the first time I picked up a racket, I was hooked. There's something about the rhythm of the game, the precision required, and the thrill of competition that keeps me coming back for more. Whether it's a casual match with friends or a competitive tournament, tennis has always been my go-to activity for fun and fitness.
Beyond the tennis court, I thrive on spending time with friends and exploring the world around me. Whether it's trying out new restaurants, embarking on spontaneous road trips, or simply hanging out and enjoying each other's company, I cherish the moments spent with my closest companions. There's a sense of adventure and camaraderie that comes with exploring new places and trying new things, and I'm always eager to dive in headfirst.
When I'm not on the court or out with friends, you'll often find me pursuing my other passion: learning about the world. Whether it's delving into the latest scientific discoveries, immersing myself in literature and art, or simply pondering life's big questions, I'm constantly seeking to expand my horizons and deepen my understanding of the world around me.
I also enjoy watching tennis tournaments on TV, my favourite tennis player is Stefanos Tsitsipas, he is greek.
